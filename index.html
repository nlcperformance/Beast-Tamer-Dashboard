The Protocols (Weeks 1-8)

Monday: The Beast

Max Strength & Neural Drive
RPE 7-8/10. Do not go to failure. Rest 3-5 mins.

1. Warm-up (5m)

Breathing + 90/90 Hips + Band Pull-aparts

2. The Main Lifts

  • A1. Wtd Tactical Pull-up 5 x 2
  • A2. Dbl KB Press 5 x 2-3
  • A3. Dbl KB Front Squat 5 x 3

3. Finisher

Heavy Suitcase Carry 3 x 30s / side

Wednesday: The Wrench

Structure & Alactic Skill (No BJJ)
Low Stress. Fix weak links. Don't chase fatigue.

1. Galpin Reset (5m)

CO2 Tolerance Breath In 4s / Hold 4s / Out 8s+

2. Pavel's Skill (15m)

Talk Test Rule: Must be able to speak clearly.

Snatch (24kg) 5L / Rest / 5R

3. Poliquin Armor (3 Rounds)

  • A1. Poliquin Step-Up 10-12 / leg
  • A2. Bottom-Up Press 5-8 / arm
  • A3. Band Pull-Apart 20 reps

4. Decompress

Dead Hang (60s total) + Box Breathing

Thursday: Capacity

SFG I Ballistics (A+A)
Focus on crisp power. If power drops, stop.

1. Tech Primer

1-Arm Snatch (Crisp) 5 x 3 / side

2. The Engine (EMOM)

Heavy 1-Arm Swings. 10 reps every minute.

Wk 1-2: 10 mins
Wk 3-4: 12 mins
Wk 5-6: 14 mins
Wk 7-8: 16 mins

3. Stabilizer

TGU (Continuous) 10 mins

4. Recovery

Box Breathing (4-4-4-4) x 5 mins

Sunday: Zone 2

Recovery & Skill

1. Cardio

Run / Ruck (Nasal Only) 45-60 mins

HR Target: 120-135 BPM

2. Grease the Groove

Pistol Squats (Practice) 3-5 singles / day

8-Week Step Loading

Wk 1
Baseline. Leave 2-3 reps in reserve.
Wk 2
Micro-load (Add 1-2kg). Extend swings.
Wk 3
Intensify. Weight up. Extend swings.
Wk 4
DELOAD. Cut sets/volume by 50%.
Wk 5-8
Repeat cycle starting heavier than Wk 1.
